WELCOME!

    Here at Movie Recommendations we use machine learning to generate personalized recommendations for you based on the movies you like. To get your recommendations create a profile and begin rating movies. The more feedback you give us the better we can tailor our recommendations to your personal tasts. We employ a technique known as collaborative filtering to make predict which movies you'll enjoy the most. By comparing your taste profile to those of other users in our database, our deep neural network looks at what movies people with similar tastes enjoyed and recommends movies that similar users rate highly.


    Although we compare ratings submitted by many users to generate our recommendations, your data remains private and no one else can see your profile or ratings. Your password is stored in encrypted form, and we will never share your data with anyone.
